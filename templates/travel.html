TRAVEL

There is a famous saying that goes as "The Journey is more important and beautiful than the Destination" , for all we know we were descendents of nomadic ancestors where survival meant keeping up with a constant move, the soul is a wanderer if we have to put it bluntly.
Travel is an emotion and a mighty topic to unravel with just words, but as what we comprehend is that a single Picture can describe and tell more than what mere words can.
I, as a photographer have tried to capture this essence and livelyness from all the few places I have traveled to not to just tell stories through my captures, instead to make you as a viewer, be part of my journey.
